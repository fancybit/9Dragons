# Integrated Setting Outline of the Nine Dragons Cultivation Series

## I. Core Worldview: Tao-Void Universe System

### 1. Cosmic Origin: The Law of Tao-Void Symbiosis

- **Definition of Tao and Void**:

  - Tao (Spiritual Essence): Life-giving energy, corresponding to the Taoist concept of "Tao follows nature," embodied as spiritual veins
  - Void (Xu Qi): Destructive energy, corresponding to the Buddhist concept of "emptiness of all phenomena," embodied as Xu caves
  - Core Law: Tao gives birth to all things, Void transforms decay, symbiosis and mutual restraint, cycle of life and death
- **Consequences of Tao-Void Imbalance**:

  - Excessive Tao: Overgrowth of living beings, disordered Tao principles
  - Excessive Void: Withering of all things, breeding of evil spirits
  - Cosmic Cataclysm: Periodic universal crisis caused by Tao-Void imbalance

### 2. Spatial Structure: From Three Realms to the Universe

#### 2.1 Main Story: Three Realms and the Nether Realm

- **Human Realm**: Mainly composed of the Qingyan Continent, including Central Domain, Eastern Domain, Western Domain, Southern Domain, Northern Domain, Nile Domain, etc.
- **Demon Realm**: A parallel space to the Qingyan Continent, inhabited by various demon clans
- **Cultivation Realm**: Main activity area for high-level cultivators, connected to the human world through special passages
- **Nether Realm**: The origin of Void energy, a channel connecting the antimatter dark matter universe

#### 2.2 Spin-off 1: Modern Blue Star Tao-Void Structure

- **Blue Star Spiritual Vein Distribution**: Core spiritual nodes in Beijing, Anyang, Kunlun, etc.
- **Tao-Void Imbalance Phenomenon**: Revival of Void energy in modern society, causing various supernatural events
- **Balance Between Cultivation World and Secular Society**: Hidden organizations maintain the boundary between the cultivation world and secular society

#### 2.3 Spin-off 2: Cosmic Tao-Void Network

- **Cosmic Tao-Void Nodes**: Distributed in galaxy cores, ancient civilization relics, interstellar dust belts
- **Differences in Cosmic Tao-Void Forms**:
  - Tao: Attached to living planets or spiritual vein planets
  - Void: Gathered in barren galaxies, black hole edges, or Xu Realm rifts
- **Space Barriers and Galactic Tao-Void Shields**: Naturally occurring Tao-Void energy barriers in the universe

### 3. Key Concepts and Phenomena

#### 3.1 Telepathy and Quantum Tunneling

- A special quantum tunneling phenomenon where quantum entanglement forms when consciousness energy is highly aligned
- Breaks through space-time barriers to achieve instant information transmission
- Theoretical basis for long-distance communication among high-level cultivators

#### 3.2 Xu Realm and Antimatter Universe

- Xu Realm is the origin of "Void" energy in the Tao-Void universe
- A channel connecting the antimatter dark matter universe
- The Black Bone Sect attempts to open the Xu Realm to trigger universal annihilation

#### 3.3 Xuanniao-MetaJade Inheritance

- Cosmic inheritance of the ancient Xuanniao civilization
- MetaJade is the inheritance carrier of Xuanniao civilization on Blue Star
- Lin Jiu (Mao Jiu/Zhu Changlan) is the core inheritor of the Xuanniao-Meta Jade inheritance

## II. Cultivation System: From Scripture Interpretation to Transcendence

### 1. Main Story: Four Realms and Twelve Stages

- **Scripture Interpretation Realm**: Preliminary comprehension of Tao principles, interpretation of scriptures, "Spiritual Essence Tribulation"

  - Four stages: Initial/Middle/Late/Peak
  - Representative characters: Dog Demon/Xing Yao, Fox Demon/Lin Qiwu
- **Tao Condensation Realm**: Condensing Tao principles within the body, forming a Tao core, "Tao Principle Tribulation"

  - Four stages: Initial/Middle/Late/Peak
  - Representative characters: Zhao Yujue/Zhao Xiaoyue, Ghost King/Gui Hua, Poison Doctor Girl/Su Xiaotang
- **Tao Transformation Realm**: Fusion of Tao principles with oneself, able to control the power of heaven and earth, "Tao-Void Tribulation"

  - Four stages: Initial/Middle/Late/Peak
  - Representative characters: Mu Qingyao/Reincarnation of Mu Guiying, Taoist Xuanqing/Abbot of Qingyun Temple, Xuanchen/Leader of Xuying Sect
- **Heaven Unity Realm**: Symbiosis with heaven and earth, Tao principles connected to the origin of heaven and earth, "Heavenly Tribulation"

  - Four stages: Initial/Middle/Late/Peak
  - Representative character: Meta Jadean/Meta Jadean Demon Lord

### 2. Spin-off 2: Interstellar Cultivation Realms

#### 2.1 Return to Origin Realm Interstellar Advancement

- **Initial Return to Origin Realm**: Break through Blue Star's space barrier, short-term travel in interstellar space
- **Middle Return to Origin Realm**: Absorb and transform stardust Tao essence, condense "Interstellar Tao Principle Domain"
- **Late Return to Origin Realm**: Control a single galaxy's Tao-Void energy nodes, domain covers the entire planet
- **Peak Return to Origin Realm (Nine Dragons Return to Origin Realm)**: Fusion of 9 Nine Dragons Tao Cores, achieving "Nine Dragons Return to Origin · Tao-Void Symbiosis"

#### 2.2 Legendary Realm: Transcendence Realm

- Controller of cosmic Tao-Void balance
- Can freely travel between the universe and Xu Realm
- Formulates cosmic Tao-Void rules
- Only the ancestor of Xuanniao civilization in ancient times ever reached this realm

### 3. Core Cultivation Rules

- **Scripture Guidance**: Advancement requires interpreting Tao principles + reading corresponding scriptures + practicing martial arts
- **Tao-Void Balance**: Cultivators must balance Tao and Void; forcing emphasis on one side will inevitably lead to backlash
- **Heavenly Tribulation Test**: Each realm advancement requires渡过 the corresponding heavenly tribulation
- **Forbidden Actions**: Evil interpretation of scriptures, destruction of spiritual veins, forced activation of killing arrays, etc.
