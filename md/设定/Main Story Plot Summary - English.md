# Nine Dragons Cultivation Story Plot Summary

## Plot Recap of First 13 Chapters

1. **Chapter 1: The "Idle Noble" Daily Life of Ninth Prince** : In the 47th year of Wanli in the Ming Dynasty (1619), 20-year-old Ninth Prince Zhu Changlan appears idle but is actually Xuanyu Zhenren, a descendant of the Snow Spirit Cat. He has a close relationship with the 37-year-old Crown Prince Zhu Changluo.
2. **Chapter 2: A Touch of Warmth in the Eastern Palace** : Zhu Changlan and the Crown Prince share a deep brotherly bond. The Crown Prince gives him a specially crafted dagger, promising to travel the rivers and lakes together in the future.
3. **Chapter 3:Dark flow at Palace Banquet, Demon Aura Revealed** : At the palace banquet, Zhu Changlan discovers a demon beside Third Prince Zhu Changxun. He tracks and kills a half-demon eunuch, and discusses countermeasures with the Crown Prince.
4. **Chapter 4: Assassination Horror, Instinct to Protect Brother** : A Black Bone Sect demon assassinates the Crown Prince. 20-year-old Zhu Changlan is seriously injured protecting his brother, and the Crown Prince takes care of him attentively.
5. **Chapter 5: Millennium Fragments in Coma** : Zhu Changlan falls into a coma after being seriously injured. His consciousness returns to millennial memories, recalling the destruction of Snow Spirit Valley, cultivation in Kunlun, etc. Finally, he wakes up at the Crown Prince's call.
6. **Chapter 6: Imperial Physician's Pulse Diagnosis, Flaws Uncovered** : The imperial physician detects abnormal pulse in Zhu Changlan. Consort Zheng takes this opportunity to make trouble, attempting to eliminate him. Zhu Changlan seeks help from Qingyun Temple, unexpectedly discovering the deep relationship between his birth mother Zhang Youxuan and Qingyun Temple, as well as the truth that she was poisoned by Consort Zheng in collusion with the Black Bone Sect. With the help of Qingyun Temple, Zhu Changlan finally proves his innocence, and Consort Zheng loses power.
7. **Chapter 7: Late-Night Talk in the Prince's Mansion, Crown Prince's Worry** : The Crown Prince worries that the Black Bone Sect's target is not only the source of Kunlun's spiritual veins but also directed at Zhu Changlan and his mother. Zhu Changlan decides to go to Kunlun Mountain to protect the spiritual veins and investigate the truth about his mother's murder.
8. **Chapter 8: Practicing Fist under the Moon, Wooden Sword Whistling** : Zhu Changlan practices martial arts diligently. The Crown Prince comes to guide him, learning that the Black Bone Sect leader's remnant soul has fled to the Ten Thousand Demon Caves in the Southern Border.
9. **Chapter 9: Fish Dried to Lure Enemies, Initial Clues** : Zhu Changlan uses fish dried to lure enemies, breaking through two Black Bone Sect strongholds and investigating the Ten Thousand Demon Caves.
10. **Chapter 10: Emperor's Indifference, Royal Alienation** : Emperor Wanli shows little concern for Zhu Changlan. Consort Zheng and Third Prince Zhu Changxun frame Zhu Changlan. Zhu Changlan resists the order to kill the Black Bone Sect leader's remnant soul and is captured by imperial guards.
11. **Chapter 11: Life Soul Bead, Dimming Light Protects Master** : Zhu Changlan recovers at Qingyun Temple. His life soul bead awakens, repelling Black Bone Sect remnants. He is appointed "Spiritual Vein Guardian Envoy.
12. **Chapter 12: Poetry Conveys Affection, Xuanyu Unsheathes** : In the 48th year of Wanli (1620), Zhu Changlan obtains the Xuanyu Miao tao in the spiritual vein forbidden area and engages in fierce battle with Black Bone Sect remnants, displaying the demeanor of "a gentleman like jade and iron.
13. **Chapter 13: Tianqi Protects the Emperor, Loyal Minister of Three Reigns** : In the 48th year of Wanli (1620), 21-year-old Zhu Changlan experiences three reign changes: Emperor Wanli's death, Crown Prince Zhu Changluo's ascension, Crown Prince Zhu Changluo's death from the "Red Pill Case," and Zhu Youxiao's ascension. His brother entrusts him on his deathbed, and he swears to protect the Ming Dynasty. Later, he suppresses the rebellion of Consort Zheng and Zhu Changxun, shatters the Black Bone Sect's conspiracy, and wholeheartedly assists Emperor Tianqi.
14. **Chapter 14: Xuanyuan Awakens, Millennium Bonds** : In the 48th year of Wanli (1620), 21-year-old Zhu Changlan goes to the Southern Border to investigate the Black Bone Sect, protects the source of spiritual veins, encounters the pressure of Xuanyuan Demon Lord's remnant soul, feels the millennium bond with Xuanyuan, and begins to explore his past life karma.
15. **Chapter 15: Seriously Injured and Comatose, Secretly Refining Spirit Fruit** : In the 48th year of Wanli (1620), Zhu Changlan uses his "serious injury and coma" as a cover to secretly refine the remaining power of the spirit fruit in his body, restoring some cat demon instincts (night vision, sense of smell, agility).

## Detailed Plot by Volume (Nine Lives Trial Arc)

### Volume 1: Mortal Prince, Nine Lives First Awakening (Chapters 13-32)

#### Core Theme: Identity Awakening and Escape from Imperial City

**Opening**: In the 47th year of Wanli in the Ming Dynasty (1619) deep in the palace, 20-year-old Ninth Prince (Mao Jiu) instinctively takes a fatal blow for the Crown Prince at the assassination scene, awakening first-life memory fragments while revealing weak demon aura.

**Development**:

- **Chapter 5: Millennium Fragments in Coma** (47th year of Wanli, 1619): 20-year-old Zhu Changlan, in his struggle with black qi, uses the power of his first life to protect his consciousness from being devoured, saving his life but losing his first life, and the power of nine lives begins to awaken.
- **Chapter 14: Xuanyuan Awakens, Millennium Bonds** (48th year of Wanli, 1620): 21-year-old Zhu Changlan goes to the Southern Border to investigate the Black Bone Sect, protects the source of spiritual veins, encounters the pressure of Xuanyuan Demon Lord's remnant soul, feels the millennium bond with Xuanyuan, and begins to explore his past life karma.
- **Chapters 15-18: Seriously Injured and Comatose, Cat Demon Instincts** (48th year of Wanli, 1620): Uses "serious injury and coma" as a cover to secretly refine the remaining power of the spirit fruit in his body, restoring some cat demon instincts (night vision, sense of smell, agility).
- **Chapters 19-23: Court Manipulation, Pretending to be Mad** (48th year of Wanli, 1620): Pretends to be mad to avoid disaster, secretly observes royal family members and court officials, discovers that Consort Zheng is colluding with demon world forces, intending to murder the Crown Prince and let Third Prince Zhu Changxun succeed.
- **Chapters 24-28: First Encounter with Fellow taoists, Small Town Breakthrough** (1st year of Tianqi, 1621): While traveling the rivers and lakes, he meets Songlan Society cultivators, gets involved in a strange disappearance case in a small town, breaks the case with basic Taoist techniques + martial arts, and initially displays his strength. In this process, he meets 26-year-old Zhao Yujue, deputy执事 of Neutral Pavilion's Guard Hall. They fight side by side and appreciate each other.
- **Chapters 29-31: Demon World Eyeline, Nine Lives Leaked** (1st year of Tianqi, 1621): A spy from an old enemy in the demon world finds the imperial palace, sneaks an attack at night. Mao Jiu uses the life soul bead from his second life to repel the enemy, but lets the secret of "nine lives" leak, forcing him to flee the capital. Zhao Yujue secretly provides help and escorts him out of the capital.

**Volume End** (Chapter 32, 1st year of Tianqi, 1621): Zhu Changlan remembers his brother Zhu Changluo's dying words, makes a promise to "protect the peace of the Ming Dynasty," takes the half "Nine Dragons Jade Pendant" (spiritual material clue) left by the Crown Prince, and embarks on the journey to find Songlan Society under Zhao Yujue's guidance.

- **Chapters 39-45: Seeking Grass in Kunlun, Third Life** (2nd year of Tianqi, 1622): According to the guidance of the Nine Dragons Jade Pendant, he goes to the edge of Kunlun Domain to find "Tranquilizing Grass," encounters a siege by low-level demons, the team coordinates, Mao Jiu uses the remaining cultivation of his third life to break the situation, losing his third life. When Zhao Yujue learns this, she is worried and goes to Kunlun Domain to visit.
- **Chapters 46-52: Old Grudge Revealed, Black Bone Sect Truth** (2nd year of Tianqi, 1622): Meets an exiled demon world cultivator, learns that the one who暗算 him back then was the demon world "Black Bone Sect," whose leader coveted the nine lives to break through realms and slaughtered Mao Jiu's clan. ## Core Setting Explanation

- **Nine Lives Mechanism**: A unique ability of Snow Spirit Cat descendants. Each time a life is lost, the bloodline power increases. Eventually, it can fuse with the origin of tao and Kong, remodeling into an eternal spirit body.
- **Nine Dragons Gathering Spirit Formation**: A sealing formation left by the ancient Creation God, requiring nine ancient spiritual objects to activate, capable of sealing the Nether Realm channel.
- **Three Realms Balance**: tao (spiritual essence) and Kong (Xu Qi) symbiotically restrict each other, maintaining the balance of the Three Realms. The Black Bone Sect attempts to break this balance and rule the Three Realms.
- **Songlan Society**: A hidden organization composed of loose cultivators, demons, and secular cultivators, dedicated to maintaining the balance of the Three Realms, and an important partner of Zhu Changlan.
