# English Synopsis

*Nine Dragon Cultivation Record* follows Zhu Changlan, the carefree Ninth Prince of the Ming Dynasty, who discovers he is the reincarnation of the ancient Meta Jade Emperor and a descendant of the Snow Spirit Cat clan. After awakening his demon powers and obtaining the mystical Meta Jade Seedlings Blade, he uncovers a conspiracy involving the evil Black Bone Sect, who seek to release the powerful Meta Hell Demon Lord.

Blending Chinese mythology, kabbalistic mysticism, and tarot wisdom, the story follows Zhu Changlan's transformation from a "foolish" prince to a wise cultivator. With the Three Realms' fate hanging in the balance, he must embrace his destiny and unite with the mysterious Songlan Society to stop the Black Bone Sect and resolve his thousand-year bond with his brothers.
